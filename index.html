About Us

Euro Standart LLC specializes in professional tile installation, focusing on stunning backsplashes and elegant bathroom designs.

With over 20 years of experience, the owner of Euro Standart LLC has been providing expert tile installation for new homes and various renovation projects. We are skilled in all aspects of tile work, including demolition, preparation, and installation, ensuring high-quality results tailored to our clients' needs.

Our team is highly trained and equipped to handle any tile-related job, from intricate custom designs to large-scale commercial projects. Whether you're looking to refresh your kitchen, bathroom, or entire home, we have the expertise to bring your vision to life.
