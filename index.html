About Us

Euro Standart LLC has been proudly serving our community for over 20 years, delivering top-notch tile installation services that bring beauty, practicality, and long-lasting durability to every project. During this time, we have completed hundreds of tile installations in kitchens, bathrooms, outdoor patios, and more, always aiming to exceed our clients’ expectations. Our mission is simple: to provide premium-quality craftsmanship at a reasonable price.

We understand that a stunning backsplash or a beautifully tiled bathroom can transform an entire space, and we take pride in offering a wide range of tile materials and design options to suit any style or budget. Whether you’re updating a single room or planning a major renovation, our experienced team will work closely with you to customize a solution that brings your vision to life in a timely manner.

Over the last two decades, we’ve built our reputation on exceptional customer service and superior workmanship. We value your trust and your home, which is why we treat every job with meticulous attention to detail. From preparation and layout to cutting and grouting, we handle each step of the process with utmost precision. Our customers appreciate not only the end results but also the clear communication, reliable scheduling, and competitive pricing we provide.

At Euro Standart LLC, we believe in doing things right the first time. Our many happy customers can attest to our commitment to quality, safety, and respect for your space. Let us show you how new tilework can elevate your home’s look and feel, all while staying within your budget and timeline. Contact us today to discuss your ideas and discover why countless homeowners continue to choose us for their tiling needs.
